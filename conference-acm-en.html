Neuro-Symbolic Compliance: Integrating LLMs and SMT Solver for Automated Financial Legal Analysis

üìç Seoul, South Korea | üèÜ ACM SIGSOFT Distinguished Paper Award

üìã Abstract

This paper introduces a neuro-symbolic approach for automated financial legal analysis and compliance verification. We combine the powerful language understanding capabilities of LLMs with the logical reasoning abilities of formal verification tools (SMT solvers) to create a system capable of understanding complex legal texts and identifying compliance issues.

The innovation of this research lies in leveraging LLM's self-correction mechanism, enabling the system to automatically verify and refine its generated logical representations. Through evaluation on financial regulatory cases, we demonstrate the superiority of our approach compared to traditional methods, earning the ACM SIGSOFT Distinguished Paper Award.
